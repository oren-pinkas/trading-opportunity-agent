larger, curative-intent population), but MRK is a poor vehicle: Padcev economics
accrue to Astellas/Pfizer, Merck's incremental exposure is a rounding error against
its ~$310B cap, and the closest empirical comp (Dec 2023 first-line EV+P approval,
a bigger and more novel event) moved MRK only ~+0.6% — inside a normal noise day.
Revised EV on MRK ≈ -0.13%; a parallel EV on PFE (out of scope) ≈ -0.09% — better
geared but still not a positive-EV trade. No equity or options position taken. See
`transcript.md` for the full three-round debate.
