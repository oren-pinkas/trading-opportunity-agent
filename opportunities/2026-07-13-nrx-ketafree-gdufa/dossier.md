Pharmaceuticals GDUFA target action date 2026-07-29 for KETAFREE, a preservative-free
IV ketamine reformulation. Reference price USD 4.16 (twelvedata, 2026-07-13T19:59Z).

Bull opened long: reformulation of an already-approved molecule carries a lower
evidentiary bar than NRx's novel CNS asset (NRX-101) track record would suggest,
and the drug addresses a recognized injectable-shortage problem, giving FDA
incentive to clear it. Proposed a small starter equity position (1-2 percent),
scaled in 2026-07-27 to 2026-07-28, riding momentum post-approval with a trailing
stop and exiting promptly on any CRL or delay. Medium confidence; flagged cash
runway, dilution risk, options liquidity, and first-cycle-versus-resubmission
status as unverified.

Bear countered that GDUFA is the generic/ANDA pathway, not a novel-drug PDUFA
review, and sterile injectable generics carry real CMC and facility-inspection
risk independent of clinical risk. NRx's own regulatory history (the NRX-101 /
RECONNECT setback) and its going-concern, reverse-split, and ATM-dilution pattern
argue that even a clean approval likely triggers an immediate dilutive raise that
caps the pop before Bull's trailing-stop plan can capture it. Proposed staying
flat pre-decision, with a narrow, small (about 1 percent), tightly-stopped idea to
fade the pop specifically after an approval print, not to short the binary event
itself.

Quant supplied the deciding numbers. Round one: central P(approval) 35 percent
(band 25-50 percent), EV tree netting to roughly negative 4 to 5 percent after
costs, breakeven approximately 37-38 percent. Round two: accepted Bear's dilution
mechanism and split the approval-scenario move into a clean-pop case (plus 55
percent, 40 percent weight) versus a pop-then-dilution-cap case (plus 25 percent,
60 percent weight), blending to plus 37 percent — this single change raised
breakeven to approximately 46 percent. Separately decomposed Bull's reformulation
argument into two orthogonal gates (clinical/pathway versus CMC/facility-inspection),
showing Bull's implied approval odds required setting the CMC gate to
approximately 1.0, which is unsupported; the two gates net to about 41 percent
even under the optimistic reformulation read. Final mixture across the ANDA-versus-
reformulation ambiguity narrowed to a synthesis point of P(approval) 33 percent
(band 25-45 percent), with the entire band sitting below the approximately 46
percent breakeven. Recommended pass, not a conviction short, because binary-catalyst
overnight gap risk forbids a naked short on a going-concern small-cap; any
expression of the negative view should be defined-risk puts only, capped at 1
percent of NAV.

All three personas converged by round two: no equity long, no naked short. Verdict:
no-trade, confidence 72. Preserved dissent for post-mortem: the CRL-scenario move
magnitude (Bull minus 20-30 percent, Quant minus 45 percent, Bear minus 55-65
