That's the real trigger for repricing bank NIM expectations.

Direction: **long/bullish on 8306.T into and through the July 31 event**, because (a) the setup is "hawkish hold" — a low bar to clear — and (b) the fundamental linkage between BOJ hikes and MUFG's earnings is direct, mechanical, and already visible in the data.

Aug 3) raises funding costs; NIM guidance shows gradual improvement (0.71% → 0.81% → 0.90%), a multi-year drip, not a July catalyst ([TradingKey](https://www.tradingkey.com/news/market-movers/261985613-market-movers-mufg-20260623)).

**4. Proposed action**
