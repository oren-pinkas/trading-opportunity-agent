   three. You cannot fade a move you cannot observe, attribute, or verify. Bull's
   "trade it relative to the live quote at execution" is a placeholder for a
   decision, not a plan, and pre-committing to buy a dip you haven't seen risks
   going long into a genuine Qatari-supply shock the tape may be pricing
   correctly.

own stop-loss condition (Iran/Qatar LNG disruption) *is* the actual catalyst Bull
